Jacks 10 Favorire Albums

Foo Fighters - Foo Fighters

This is the first studio album by the band Foo Fighters. Released in the summer of 1995, just a year or so after the death of Nirvana singer Kurt Coabin, Nirvana drummer Dave Grohl rented 2 weeks of studio time to make his ultimate passion project. This album quickly became one of my favorites. it mixes the grunge sound with a more punk/alternative influince. My favorite songs off the album are "Good Greif" and "Exhausted.""
